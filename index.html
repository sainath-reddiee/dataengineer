About the Author

DataEngineer Hub is written and maintained by Sainath Reddy, a practicing data engineer with hands-on experience building production data pipelines on Snowflake, AWS, dbt, and Apache Airflow. Every tutorial on this site is based on real-world engineering work — not reposted material — and is reviewed before publication for technical accuracy.

Our editorial focus is narrow and deep: cloud data warehousing (Snowflake, BigQuery), data transformation (dbt, SQL), orchestration (Airflow), and modern data platform best practices. We publish in-depth tutorials, comparison guides, and reference material for engineers shipping data in production.
