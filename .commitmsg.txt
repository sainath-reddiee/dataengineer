seo: close schema and social-card gaps on PSEO pages (Phase 1)

Before this change, ~200+ programmatic SEO pages (Comparison, Glossary,
CheatSheet + 3 hubs) bypassed the central MetaTags component and were
shipping without og:image, twitter:image, BreadcrumbList JSON-LD, or
FAQPage JSON-LD. Result: no social-card preview on LinkedIn/Twitter/
Slack/Discord, no breadcrumb or FAQ rich snippets in Google SERPs.

Changes:
- ComparisonPage: full OG/Twitter image suite, BreadcrumbList JSON-LD,
  FAQPage JSON-LD when comparison.faqs present.
- GlossaryPage: full OG/Twitter image suite, BreadcrumbList from
  derivedData.breadcrumbs, FAQPage JSON-LD when term.faqs present.
- CheatSheetPage: full OG/Twitter image suite; Article schema author
  upgraded Organization -> Person with url + sameAs; added image
  ImageObject, publisher.logo ImageObject, mainEntityOfPage WebPage
  (required for Google rich results).
- CheatSheetHubPage, ComparisonHubPage, GlossaryHubPage: full OG/Twitter
  image suite (og:image width/height/alt, site_name, locale, twitter
  site/creator). ComparisonHubPage + GlossaryHubPage now emit
  BreadcrumbList JSON-LD.
- seoConfig.getArticleSchema: optional wordCount parameter emitted only
  when > 0.

Build verified (2043 modules, 0 errors). Spot-checked dist/compare/
index.html contains og:image, twitter:image, breadcrumb schema.
